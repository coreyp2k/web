Company Overview


A.I.S., Inc (AIS) is unwavering in its commitment to protect our nation's natural resources and their habitats. We conduct sound science and uitilize an ecosystem-based approach to environmental management, providing indispensable services for the nation. Our mission is firmly grounded in sustainability, driving us to continually enhance our methods for monitoring the world's oceans, coastal communities, and global fisheries. Operating 24/7, we deliver high-quality, unbiased data, observations, samples, regulatory support, and research to our customers, empowering them to maintain decision superiority.
