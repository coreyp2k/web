Company Overview


A.I.S., Inc (AIS) supports the stewardship of our nation's ocean resources and their habitats. We provide vital services for the nation utilizing sound science and an ecosystem-based approach to environmental management. Our mission is steeped in sustainability. We strive to coninuously evolve our approach to monitoring the world's oceans, coastal communities, and global fisheries. We stand watch 24/7, providing our customers with the pragmatic science and environmental intelligence they need to maintain decision superiority.
