Electronic Monitoring
Services and Solutions

Electronic monitoring services (EMS) is fast becoming an alternative to onboard fisheries observation and may offers cost savings and risk reductions while still meeting the data collection standards of most domestic and international fisheries observation programs. AIS has developed a robust EMS solution that is currently providing services to both domestic and international clients. AIS works with industry leading providers of hardware and software that leverage wireless transfer of video data as well as a cloud-based storage system. AIS offers all aspects of the EMS including.

- Consultation for EM project design
- Project management
- Vessel Monitoring Plan (VMP) development
- EM reviewer training (program specific)
- Onboard EM system installation, training, and support
- Video data review and database management
- Data analysis and reporting
- QA/QC and third-party verification
- Direct data submission through programming interfaces

Electronic Monitoring System Installation

AIS works with electronic monitoring hardware and software providers each with their own unique equipment capabilities. We provide a best value tradeoff for our clients when determining the best system for the project requirements and data being collected, taking into consideration the equipment requirements of the regional government program it will be operating in. We utilize local marine installers to ensure systems are professionally installed and have the durability required to sustain long term uninterrupted use, yet maintenance support is nearby

Trained Reviewers

Our EM reviewers are selected from our internal pool of employees many of whom are certified by NMFS and operate under a regulatory NOAA Fisheries EM program certified by the regional EM program. Employees are trained on the review software, program objectives, fish species and all other technical aspects of each program. Our EM review team is bolstered by strong field and fisheries backgrounds, bringing the attention to detail and biology training necessary for efficient review.

Project Design and Management

All data collected is encrypted, stored, and managed in a Cloud environment that meets or exceed all security requirements to protect all sensitive or proprietary data
Advantages of our solution:

- Dedicated experienced project management and video review team.
- GPS tracking and secure, encrypted data transfer.
- Wireless EM video data transfer through cellular, Wi-Fi or Starlink satellite connections. No hard drives to mail, which lowers the rise of video data being compromised, delayed, or lost.
- Clients access live streaming from all vessel cameras via Starlink satellite connectivity system.
- High speed internet connectivity onboard vessel available to captain and crew for market updates, access to emergency safety personnel, real time weather and personal devices for e-mail and other communication.
- Machine vision algorithms identify Critical Tracking Events (CTEs)—such as catch being brought onboard or man overboard.
- Communications packages tailored for small, mid, and large-scale fishing vessels—with industry leading price/performance.
- Data is provided to operators and government regulators in industry standard data formats via secure APIs.

NOAA Fisheries Approved Provider

AIS is an approved EMS provider for the NOAA Fisheries Greater Atlantic Regional Fisheries Office and the NMFS West Coast Region Pacific Coast Groundfish Program. We have international experience in the following countries:
- Gulf of Guinea (Ghana)
- South Pacific Ocean (Chile)
- Indian Ocean (Kenya)
- Caribbean Sea (Panama)
